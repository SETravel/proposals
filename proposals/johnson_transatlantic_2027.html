BARCELONA ITINERARY — MAY 25–28
Day 1May 25
Arrival & The Gothic Quarter
Arrive at BCN and check in to your hotel. Slow afternoon stroll through the Barri Gòtic — cobblestone lanes, Roman ruins, and the magnificent Barcelona Cathedral. Let the evening unfold with cava and tapas on a sidewalk terrace.
Day 2May 26
Gaudí Day & Dinner at Lasarte
Pre-booked morning at the Sagrada Família. Afternoon at Casa Batlló or La Pedrera on Passeig de Gràcia. Evening at Lasarte (3 Michelin stars) — Chef Martín Berasategui's crown jewel and one of Spain's finest tables.
Day 3May 27
Barceloneta, Picasso & Last Evening
Morning at the Picasso Museum in El Born, then Barceloneta Beach for a long Mediterranean lunch. Afternoon — Montjuïc by cable car, the Boqueria market, or a spa treatment. Final evening tapas crawl through El Born.
Day 4May 28
Embarkation — Norwegian Aura
Relaxed morning checkout, then transfer to Port of Barcelona (~20 min) to board the brand-new Norwegian Aura for her inaugural transatlantic crossing. Raise a glass in The Haven Bar as the Barcelona skyline fades behind you.

CRUISE ITINERARY — MAY 28 – JUNE 10, 2027
Day 1May 28
Embarkation — Barcelona
Board at Port of Barcelona and settle into your Haven Penthouse with Balcony #15824. Explore Ocean Heights, the Ocean Boulevard promenade, and The Haven's private sundeck and pool. Sail away as the sun sets behind Montjuïc.
Day 2May 29
Motril (Granada), Spain
Gateway to Granada and the incomparable Alhambra palace — use your included excursion credits here. A rare bucket-list opportunity not to be missed. Alternatively explore the white-washed village of Nerja.
Day 3May 30
Cádiz (Seville), Spain
Consider the full-day excursion to Seville — the Cathedral, the Alcázar, and the Barrio Santa Cruz — or explore Cádiz itself with its golden cathedral dome and rooftop terrace views over two seas.
Day 4May 31
Gibraltar, U.K.
The Rock guards the mouth of the Mediterranean. Cable car to the top for views across to Africa, meet the famous Barbary macaques, explore St. Michael's Cave, and browse duty-free Main Street below.
Days 5–13Jun 1–9
Nine Days at Sea — The Atlantic Crossing
The heart of the transatlantic experience. Haven guests enjoy exclusive access to a private pool deck, sundeck, restaurant, lounge, and bar. Use your 4 included specialty dining meals at Cagney's Steakhouse or Onda by Scarpetta. Ocean Heights waterslides by day; Broadway-caliber entertainment by night.
Day 14Jun 10
Arrival — Miami, Florida
Sail into PortMiami with the downtown skyline ahead. Haven guests receive priority disembarkation. Ground transport to airport or hotel is included in your package.
