Getting There & Back

Transatlantic Flights

Blake & Brenda from Nashville (BNA). Cecilia & Matt from Cincinnati (CVG). Everyone lands Rome Jul 22, flies home from Amsterdam Aug 1.

Open-Jaw Tickets
US → FCO / AMS → US
~$1,400–1,800/pp
This is the biggest line item. Peak-season late July open-jaw from mid-size US airports runs $1,400–1,800/pp. That's reality.

From Nashville: Delta (via ATL/JFK), United (via EWR), British Airways (via LHR), KLM. Check Google Flights with +/- 3 day flex.
From Cincinnati: Delta (via DTW/JFK), United (via EWR/IAD), Air Canada (via YYZ).

Tips: Try booking each leg separately and mixing airlines. Use Google Flights "explore" and Skiplagged for creative routing. Every week you wait costs money — book now.

🛏️
ÖBB Nightjet NJ236 — Venezia Santa Lucia → München Ost
Departs 21:05 · Arrives Munich ~05:00 · Hop off at 5 AM · Alps overnight
~€50–70/pp couchette
The 5 AM Munich Play:
Board 9 PM after your last Venice dinner. 4-berth couchette — just your crew. Fresh sheets, pillow, blanket, door locks. Sleep through Udine, over the Alps, past Salzburg. The train stops at München Ost around 5 AM — alarm, bags, off. Stash bags in station lockers (€3–7), grab a coffee and fresh pretzel at a Bäckerei, and you own the entire day.

Private 4-berth couchette: ~€200–260 total (~€50–65/pp). Book at oebb.at or nightjet.com — opens 180 days ahead. Summer fills up.

You'll miss the included breakfast (served after Munich). Bring snacks or plan to eat at the station.

Pro Tips

Make It Count

📱
eSIM
Airalo or Holafly, ~$20–30 for 11 days EU data. Non-negotiable for maps, translation, bookings.
🎒
Pack light
40L backpack + small daypack. You move every 1–2 days. Ryanair bag fees add up fast. Quick-dry clothes, rewear jeans, do laundry in Stuttgart.
💳
No-fee cards
Schwab debit (no ATM fees) + Chase Sapphire or Cap One (no FTF). Always decline "convert to USD" at terminals.
🎫
Book ahead
Colosseum + Vatican (sold out in July), Anne Frank House (6 wks), Nightjet (summer = full). Everything else is walkup.
🍷
Eat & drink well
Lunch at markets/street food, dinner sit-down with wine. House wine in Italy €3–5/glass. Germany: beer < water. Amsterdam: splurge the last night.
🚂
Train apps
Trenitalia + Italo (compare both), ÖBB (Nightjet), DB Navigator (Germany). Buy in-app. Screenshot QR codes offline.
🎓 Booking Priority
1. Transatlantic flights NOW — open-jaw: US→FCO + AMS→US
