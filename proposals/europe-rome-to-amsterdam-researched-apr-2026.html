Pro Tips

Make It Count

📱
eSIM
Airalo or Holafly, $20–30 for 11 days. Essential for maps, translation, bookings across 3 countries.
🎒
Pack light
40L backpack + daypack. You move 1–2 days per city. Ryanair bag fees add up. Quick-dry clothes.
💳
No-fee cards
Schwab debit (no ATM fees) + Chase Sapphire (no FTF). Always decline "convert to USD" at terminals.
🎫
Book ahead
Colosseum, Vatican (sold out July). Anne Frank House (6 weeks). Nightjet (summer = full). Everything else: walkup.
🍷
Eat & drink well
Lunch: markets/street food. Dinner: sit-down with wine. Italy: €3–5/glass wine. Germany: beer costs less than water.
🚂
Train apps
Trenitalia + Italo (compare), ÖBB (Nightjet), DB Navigator (Germany). Screenshot QR codes offline.
